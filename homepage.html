About Us

The Career Express Club (CEC) is a student-led organization at Multimedia University (MMU) dedicated to helping students prepare for their future careers. We organize various events, workshops, and industrial visits to provide students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the professional world.

Vision

Bridging the gap between academic learning and industry demands, empowering students with skills and opportunities that enhance their employability and career readiness.

Mission

Enhance Employability: Equipping students with employability skills to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world applications.
